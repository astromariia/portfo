002 : Artificial Intelligence Garbage Sorter

In recycling plants, paper, cardboard, and glass are usually sorted by hand since glass is delicate and paper and cardboard are flimsy. To solve this problem, I trained a YOLO model to sort trash and recyclables making recycling plants more efficient.

"Only 9% of the plastic ever produced has been recycled, and 19% has been incinerated."

Douglas Main. MIT Technology Review.

My model perceiving a plastic waterbottle.

The UI I have built still has difficulties recognizing objects in a pile of trash.

The UI I built gives you advice on how to recycle a given object.
